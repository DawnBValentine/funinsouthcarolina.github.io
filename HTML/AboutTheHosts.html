Mrs. Dawn Valentine

Born and raised in Aiken, South Carolina, Mrs. Valentine is a life long South Carolina Resident. Aside from living in the Aiken Area, she also has relatives scattered across the state from Summerville, to Orangeburg, Greenwood, Beaufort, and everywhere in between. Her favorite family tradition is vacationing in Edisto Beach each summer. Over the years, she has traveled, not only to visit with family, but also to the various state parks, lakes, aquariums and historical monument. She's been to festicals and fairs in countless counties, and she's participated in events both in her childhood and as a parent herself. She is excited to share her expertise and love with the palmetto state with visitors and locals alike.

Miss Emily Campbell

My name is Emily Campbell and I am an undergraduate student at the University of South Carolina studying Elementary Education. I have lived in South Carolina for the 20 years that have been my life. I have explored every corner of it from Hunting Island to the mountains of Oconee National Park. I absolutely love this state I get to call home and I cannot wait to share all the lovely places South Carolina has to offer. My favorite place in South Carolina would be my very own backyard since I currently reside on the beautiful Lake Murray.
