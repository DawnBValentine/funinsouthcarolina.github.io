About Our Site

On this website, we are excited to offer both locals and tourists alike a taste of the very best of what South Carolina has to offer. While many sites aim to provide exhaustive lists of happenings around the state, our site focuses in on key events and locations that we feel best represent our state.

In the content section, you will find podcasts and vodcasts covering various topics including attractions around the state and holiday events! Look for our Monthly Fun Calendar on the Event Calendar page. This is where we highlight all of our favorite events each month! Finally, you can submit your event, resturant, or attration to our experts for their review on the contact page!
